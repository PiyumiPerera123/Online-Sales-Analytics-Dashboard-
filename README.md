#  Online Sales Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

##  Overview
This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard built using an e-commerce dataset from Kaggle. The goal is to analyze online sales performance and uncover key business insights to support data-driven decision-making.

---

##  Objective
- Analyze revenue trends and customer behavior  
- Identify top-performing products and categories  
- Understand regional and seasonal sales patterns  
- Support strategic business decisions in e-commerce  

---

##  Tools & Technologies
- Power BI  
- Excel (Data Preparation)  
- Kaggle Dataset  
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

##  Key Insights
-  Female customers contribute **62.17%** of total revenue  
-  Revenue increases significantly towards year-end (seasonal trend)  
-  Office products are the top-selling category  
-  Chicago and California generate the highest revenue  
-  Most purchases occur at lower price points (price-sensitive customers)  
-  Customer retention lasts up to 36 months before decline  

---

##  Conclusion
This dashboard helps identify key business trends in customer behavior, sales performance, and product demand, enabling better marketing strategies and decision-making.
